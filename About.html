About Rim Jhim Restaurant

Rim Jhim Restaurant is a trusted food destination located near Gandhi School in Hata, Kushinagar, Uttar Pradesh. We proudly serve fresh, hygienic, and flavorful meals to families, students, and visitors in the local community.

Our menu blends traditional Indian cuisine with popular fast-food favorites. Whether you are dining with friends or enjoying a family evening, we focus on quality ingredients, authentic taste, and a comfortable ambience.
